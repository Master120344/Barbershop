ABOUT CLEAN CUTZ

Discover the passion, artistry, and community behind every cut.

Anthony

Owner & Master Barber

Anthony is not just a barber; he's an artist and designer with a passion for creating unique styles. Based in the heart of Austin, Texas, his creative vision extends to custom shirt designs, reflecting his dedication to craftsmanship and personal expression. Experience the artistry in every cut and design. He founded Clean Cutz with the vision of creating a space where style, skill, and community converge.

Our Philosophy

At Clean Cutz, we believe grooming is more than just a haircut; it's a form of self-expression and confidence building. We are committed to providing an exceptional client experience, combining traditional barbering techniques with modern styling trends. Our studio is designed to be a welcoming space for everyone, fostering a sense of community and belonging. We use only high-quality products to ensure the best results for your hair and skin.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver unparalleled grooming services that enhance our clients' personal style and well-being. We strive for excellence in every aspect of our craft, from the precision of our cuts to the warmth of our service. We aim to be Austin's premier barbershop, known for our skill, creativity, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
